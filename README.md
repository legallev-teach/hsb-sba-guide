# HSB SBA Student Guide
**Caribbean Union College Secondary School**  
Human and Social Biology — School-Based Assessment Resources

---

## What this is

An interactive HTML guide built for CSEC HSB students completing their School-Based Assessments. It walks students through every stage of the SBA process in plain language, with screenshots and worked examples.

Designed for mobile-first viewing since most students will access it on their phones.

---

## Contents

| File | Purpose |
|---|---|
| `index.html` | Main student guide — setup, document structure, analysis formula, submission |
| `assets/` | All screenshots and images referenced in the guide |

---

## Topics covered in the guide

- Installing Google Docs and creating your document
- Naming your file in the correct format
- Group-to-individual workflow
- Presentation of data — chart types, tables, titles and descriptions
- Writing your analysis — a 5-step formula with sentence starters
- How to share your document with your teacher

---

## Who this is for

Students in CSEC Human and Social Biology at Caribbean Union College Secondary School, Trinidad and Tobago.
